CSS Gradient Generator

Webpage designing is an interesting part of frontend development.

Gradients basically mean changing of magnitude of a property with change in position.CSS gives us a way to apply gradients in background-image property of styles.This is a minimalistic and simple tool to understand linear and radial gradient.
You have to fill the three input forms with your favourite colors(or their hexcodes) and just press the button to get to see how the gradients would look and the respective CSS codes.
